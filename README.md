# 🐸 Progress Pond V24

A cozy, frog-themed wellness + diabetes tracking dashboard designed to visualize the relationship between:

* 🩸 Glucose trends
* 😊 Mood states
* 💧 Hydration
* 🥣 Carbs
* 💉 Insulin
* 🧠 Stress
* ⚡ Energy
* 🩺 Symptoms
* 🏃 Exercise
* 🌿 Daily productivity

Built as a personal health analytics project focused on the *human experience* behind chronic illness management.

---

# ✨ Features

## 📈 Multi-Layer Trend Graph

Interactive Chart.js visualization combining:

* glucose readings
* mood tracking
* hydration
* carb intake
* insulin doses
* stress levels
* energy levels

Designed to reveal correlations between:

* emotional state
* glucose variability
* behavioral patterns

---

## 🐸 Mood Tracking

Track emotional states with emoji-based inputs:

* 😊 Happy
* 😌 Calm
* 🧐 Focused
* 😴 Tired
* 😠 Grumpy
* 😕 Confused
* 😡 Angry
* 😢 Sad
* 😭 Crying
* 🤪 Manic

Mood entries are graphed over time to compare against glucose trends.

---

## 🩸 Diabetes Tracking

### Log:

* glucose readings
* carb intake
* insulin doses
* hydration

Supports:

* trend visualization
* variability tracking
* pattern discovery

---

## 🧠 Behavioral Health Metrics

Additional tracking for:

* stress
* energy
* symptoms
* exercise

This allows deeper analysis of:

* emotional regulation
* fatigue
* burnout
* cognitive performance
* physical activity effects

---

## 🌿 Daily Productivity ("Hops")

Task tracking system with:

* priority levels
* progress bars
* achievement history

Gamified to feel supportive rather than clinical.

---

## 📊 Daily Analytics

Automatic calculations:

* average glucose
* highs/lows
* stability score
* trend visualization

---

## 💾 Local Storage Persistence

All data is saved locally in-browser using:

```javascript
localStorage
```

No account required.

No backend required.

---

## 📁 Excel Export

Export graphs + tracking data into downloadable `.xlsx` reports using:

* ExcelJS
* FileSaver.js

# 🐸 Philosophy

Progress Pond is built around the idea that:

> health data should feel compassionate, not punishing.

The goal is to create a space where tracking feels:

* encouraging
* reflective
* emotionally aware
* visually calming

instead of overwhelming.
